Mutton Pulao

Recipe

Start by making Pulao Masala! ... Coarsely pound a small piece of ginger and a couple of green chilies. ... In a deep pan, heat 2-3 tbsp ghee. ... In the same pan, add whole spices: ... Add 1 sliced onion and some salt. ... Stir in 1.5 cups mixed veggies (carrots, cauliflower, green peas, etc.), followed by 1.5 cups soaked basmati rice.
