How to make homemade beef kebab? Beef mince kebabs recipe - BBC Food Method. Mix the beef, cumin, paprika, salt and plenty of freshly ground black pepper together in a bowl. Divide the mince mixture into 8 portions and press each portion firmly onto flat skewers, each one around 10–12cm/4–4½in long. Place a non-stick griddle pan over a high heat and brush with a little oil.
