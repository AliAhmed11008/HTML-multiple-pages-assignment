Chicken Biryani

Recipe

Heat the oil in a frying pan, add the shallots, onions, garlic and ginger and fry for a couple of minutes, until golden-brown. Remove a tablespoonful and set aside for garnishing. Add the chicken and stir fry for four minutes. Add the spices and stir for one minute, then stir in the yoghurt and simmer for 1-2 minutes.
